Welcome to the Freefall Uni Madr. Lean to skydive in just 3 days.

Train with Us Using Wind Tunnels and Sky

Whether you aspire to learn to skydive on an AFF course or progress your skills in your chosen discipline we can help. Using the latest teaching techniques combining wind tunnels and the sky take advantage of our hybrid courses.

Formation Skydiving

Using the wind tunnel and the sky we can help you achieve your goals. For example you might wish to get your FS1 using tunnel and sky. Maybe you want to be part of a rookie team or need more custom AAA team training. Talk to us about tunnel and sky combined.

Freefly training

We can encompass the BPA FF1 FF2 requirements in cutomized tunnel and sky training. Mix it up for max progression.

Total Flight Canopy School
